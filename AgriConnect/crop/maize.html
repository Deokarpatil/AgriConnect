Disease Management

Turcicum Leaf Blight

Symptoms: Long, elliptical lesions on leaves

Treatment:
  • Use resistant varieties
  • Proper spacing
  • Fungicide application

Common Rust

Symptoms: Small, circular pustules on leaves

Treatment:
  • Good air circulation
  • Fungicide spray
  • Crop rotation

Stalk Rot

Symptoms: Soft, discolored stalks

Treatment:
  • Proper drainage
  • Disease-free seed
  • Balanced fertilization
