Cultivation Guide

Land Preparation

  • Deep ploughing (30-40 cm)
  • Fine tillage for good soil structure
  • Field leveling for irrigation
  • Furrow preparation

Planting

  • Select disease-free setts
  • Treat with fungicides
  • Plant in furrows
  • Maintain proper spacing

Water Management

  • Regular irrigation
  • Drainage management
  • Water stress prevention
  • Moisture monitoring

Fertilizer Management

Recommended Dosage

Nitrogen (N) 250-300 kg/ha
Phosphorus (P) 100-150 kg/ha
Potassium (K) 150-200 kg/ha
Zinc (Zn) 25 kg/ha

Disease Management

Red Rot

Symptoms: Red patches on stem, internal tissue turning red

Treatment:
  • Use resistant varieties
  • Hot water treatment of setts
  • Remove infected plants

Smut

Symptoms: Black whip-like structures from growing points

Treatment:
  • Plant certified seed
  • Remove infected plants
  • Crop rotation

Ratoon Stunting

Symptoms: Stunted growth, thin stalks

Treatment:
  • Hot water treatment
  • Clean farm equipment
  • Disease-free planting material

Harvesting Guide

When to Harvest

  • Crop age: 12-18 months
  • Brix reading: 18-20%
  • Yellowing of leaves
  • Dry weather conditions

Harvesting Methods

  • Manual harvesting
  • Mechanical harvesting
  • Proper cutting height
  • Quick transportation
