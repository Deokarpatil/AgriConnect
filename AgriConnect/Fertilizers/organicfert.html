Organic Farming

Organic farming is a method of farming that relies on natural processes, biodiversity, and cycles adapted to local conditions. It avoids the use of synthetic chemicals and genetically modified organisms.

Benefits of Organic Farming

  • Improves soil fertility and structure.
  • Promotes biodiversity and ecological balance.
  • Reduces pollution and conserves water.
  • Produces healthier and more nutritious crops.

Popular Organic Fertilizers

  • Compost
  • Manure
  • Bone Meal
  • Seaweed
